Scrambled Egg

  • Description
  • Ingredients
  • Directions
  • Cooking

Description

Scrambled eggs is a dish made from eggs (usually chicken eggs) stirred, whipped or beaten together while being gently heated, typically with salt, butter, oil and sometimes other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • salt
  • pepper
  • cayenne pepper

Directions

  1. heat nonstick pan on medium-high heat
  2. melt butter til brown on medium heat
  3. crack eggs and stir lightly, but consistently
  4. while stirring, alternate between keeping the pan on/off the stovetop
  5. add salt, pepper, cayenne pepper to taste and desired spice level
